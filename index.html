The Problem with Diesel Boats

Environmental Impact

Diesel engines contribute to air pollution by releasing CO₂ and NOₓ, harming ecosystems.

Noise Pollution

Diesel engines disrupt the tranquility of the waterways, making the experience less peaceful.

The Rise of Electric Canal Boats

Electric boats offer a cleaner, quieter, and more sustainable future for canal travel.
